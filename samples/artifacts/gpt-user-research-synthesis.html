GPT synthesis · 12 interviews · Product & Design teams

AI Artifact Sharing — Research Themes

1. Link rot is the #1 pain

"I paste Figma and Claude links in Slack and nobody can find them a week later."

9/12 participants described lost context when links expire or threads scroll away.

2. Review wants structure, not threads

Stakeholders prefer comment threads attached to the artifact over Slack reactions.

3. Browse beats search (until catalog grows)

Gallery + tags sufficient under ~50 items; semantic search becomes critical after that.

4. MCP curiosity is high

Engineers want Claude Desktop to publish without leaving the IDE conversation.
